EXPLORATORY PROJECTS

A collection of smaller personal projects that explore various ideas and technologies. These are rapid prototypes, experiments, or tools that may not warrant a full project page but are still worth sharing.

Prototype: Machine Learning Bot

Simple robot that senses external environment and reacts base on a simplified Q-learning algorithm. (2025)

Experiment: Welding

Learned and practiced stick welding with mostly E7018/E6013 sticks (with professional guidance and proper PPE). (2024)

Prototype: improvised Speaker

Built a speaker from a copper coil and a papercup. Now I can blast music by connecting it to the standard 3.5mm audio port. (2023)

Experiment: RC Circuits

Explored the behavior of resistor-capacitor circuits and their response to step inputs, as part of ELECENG 2E04 course. (2023)

Prototype: Snowboarding training rail

As requested by my friend, I built a durable snowboarding training rail to help practice technique and balance even at home. (2022)

Project: Motorcycle Restoration

Restored a disabled 1999 Suzuki GS125 motorcycle, purchased from a scrapyard for $120, so I can learn to ride motorcycle. (2021)

Project: Lawn Mower Restoration

Restored a disabled 150cc lawn mower, purchased as scrap for $25. I used it for yard work during covid lockdown, and earned ~$300 extra cash by helping neighbors. (2021)

Prototype: Sterling Engine

Made a Sterling external combustion engine. (2018)

Prototype: Blast Furnace

Melted and casted ingots of aluminum, silver and copper with the improvised ~1200°C coke blast furnace. (2018)

Experiments: Rocketry

Explored rocketry with A6-3/B6-4 solid engines, compressed air, and water propulsion systems. (2017)
